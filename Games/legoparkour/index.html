Lego Parkour


A Fun game to play where you as a Lego minifigure have a major objective to collect 2 crabs, 15 jewels and 1 fruit to win and remember to stay on your toes! The game is controlled by arrow keys, space to jump and mouse to look you can also see controls in main menu which appears after the 1st game. pro tip: Many objects are interactive and you do not know what secrets are in there Good Luck!!
